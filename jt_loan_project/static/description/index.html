Overview

Welcome to our state-of-the-art Loan Processing System, a one-stop solution designed to streamline and simplify the loan application and approval process. With a user-friendly interface and a wide range of features, our system offers a seamless experience for both customers and officers involved in the lending process.



From free sign-up for public users to a custom-designed email verification system,customer credit score check, our platform ensures the highest level of security and convenience. As a user, you will have access to a variety of roles, including approver officer, credit officer, finance officer, system officer, collection officer, legal officer, and customer, with flexible permissions tailored to your specific needs.



Moreover, we have implemented an intelligent project screen with predefined stages to track all your recent activities effortlessly. With automated calculations for loan installments based on current interest rates, a user dashboard for verification purposes, and streamlined document submission and approval processes, you will enjoy a hassle-free journey from application to loan disbursement.



Additionally, our integrated video attestation feature allows for easy scheduling of appointments via Google Meet, ensuring a secure and personalized experience. To finalize the loan approval, our system offers an electronic signature method, making it a completely paperless process. Rest assured that our user manual will guide you through every step, making your loan application journey simple, efficient, and successful. Get ready to explore the full potential of our Loan Processing System, bringing the world of financial opportunities to your fingertips from the comfort of your home.

Features

Free Sign-Up: Public users can create accounts without any cost, enabling quick access to the loan application process.

Custom Email Verification System: A secure email verification process to ensure the authenticity of user accounts.

Role-Based Access: Multiple roles, including approver officer, credit officer, finance officer, system officer, collection officer, legal officer, and customer, each with tailored permissions.

Intelligent Project Screen: Predefined stages to track and manage loan application progress effectively.

Automated Loan Installment Calculations: Calculation of loan installments based on current interest rates, providing transparency to users.

User Dashboard: A dedicated dashboard for users to monitor and verify their application status.

Integrated Video Attestation: Easy scheduling of appointments via Google Meet, offering secure and personalized interaction.

Electronic Signature Method: Paperless approval process through electronic signatures, saving time and resources.

Flexible Permissions: Customizable permissions for different roles to ensure data access is appropriate and controlled.

Seamless Loan Disbursement: Smooth process from application to loan disbursal, minimizing delays and complexities.

Convenient Remote Access: Accessible from the comfort of users' homes, making the loan application process convenient and efficient.

Transparent Application Tracking: Clear visibility into the status of applications, reducing uncertainty and anxiety for users.
